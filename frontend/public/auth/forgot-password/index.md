## Reset Your Password

Forgot your password? Enter the email address associated with your MarocSphere account and we'll send you a password reset link. The link expires in 1 hour.

### Steps:
1. Enter your registered email address
2. Check your inbox for the reset email
3. Click the reset link
4. Choose a new password
