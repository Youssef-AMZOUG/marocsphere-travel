## Morocco Travel Safety

MarocSphere provides real-time safety monitoring for all major Moroccan cities and tourist areas. Our safety scores are calculated from multiple data sources including local authorities, traveler reports, and verified news.

---

## Safety Features

### Real-Time Safety Score
A 0-100 safety gauge updated every 15 minutes. Scores above 80 indicate safe conditions, 60-80 require caution, and below 60 suggest elevated risk.

### Emergency SOS
One-tap emergency SOS button with GPS location sharing. Connects directly to local emergency services.

### Regional Alerts
Active safety alerts and advisories for specific regions and cities across Morocco.

## Safety Tips

- Always use official, licensed guides (government-issued badge)
- Insist on taxi meters (compteur) or agree on price before riding
- Keep valuables secure in crowded souks and markets
- Drink bottled water only
- Download offline maps for the Fes medina
- Use MarocSphere's safety dashboard for real-time updates
