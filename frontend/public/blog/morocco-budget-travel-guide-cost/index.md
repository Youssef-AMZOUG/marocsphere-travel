## Morocco on a Budget: Complete Cost Breakdown

Detailed Morocco travel costs breakdown. Budget accommodation, food, transport, and activities. Travel Morocco for under $50/day.

## Daily Budget Breakdown

### Budget Traveler ($30-50/day)
- **Accommodation:** Hostel dorm (80-150 MAD) or budget riad (200-300 MAD)
- **Food:** Street food and local restaurants (100-150 MAD/day)
- **Transport:** CTM buses and shared taxis (50-100 MAD/day average)
- **Activities:** Free medina walks, mosque exteriors, markets

### Mid-Range ($80-150/day)
- **Accommodation:** Nice riad (400-800 MAD)
- **Food:** Mix of local and restaurant dining (200-350 MAD/day)
- **Transport:** Private transfers and taxis (200-400 MAD/day)
- **Activities:** Guided tours, cooking classes, hammam

### Luxury ($200-500/day)
- **Accommodation:** 5-star riad or hotel (1500-5000+ MAD)
- **Food:** Fine dining (500-1000 MAD/day)
- **Transport:** Private car and driver (800-1500 MAD/day)
- **Activities:** Exclusive experiences, private guides

## Sample Prices (2026)

- Orange juice: 4-5 MAD
- Tagine at local restaurant: 35-60 MAD
- Mint tea: 10-15 MAD
- CTM bus Marrakech-Fes: 200 MAD
- Petit taxi ride: 10-30 MAD
- Museum entry: 20-70 MAD
- Hammam: 100-600 MAD
- Cooking class: 350-500 MAD
- Camel trek: 300-800 MAD

## Budget Tips

1. Eat where locals eat (follow the queues)
2. Use CTM buses between cities (reliable and cheap)
3. Bargain everything — start at 30% of asking price
4. Free activities: medina walks, gardens, beach
5. Travel in shoulder season (Sep-Nov, Mar-May)
