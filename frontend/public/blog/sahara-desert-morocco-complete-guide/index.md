## Sahara Desert Morocco: Complete Guide

From Merzouga to Zagora, everything about Sahara desert tours. Camel treks, desert camps, best operators, and what to pack for the dunes.

## Merzouga vs Zagora

**Merzouga (Erg Chebbi):** Taller dunes (up to 150m), more dramatic landscape, better desert camps. The classic Sahara experience. Recommended.

**Zagora (Erg Chigaga):** More remote, fewer tourists, wilder landscape. Longer drive from Marrakech.

## What to Expect

- Camel trek into the dunes (1-2 hours)
- Sunset and sunrise over the Sahara
- Desert camp with traditional music and dinner
- Stargazing (no light pollution)
- Sandboarding and quad biking (optional)

## Desert Camp Options

- **Budget:** Shared tents, basic facilities (200-400 MAD/person)
- **Mid-range:** Private tents, hot showers, good food (500-1000 MAD/person)
- **Luxury:** Glamping with king beds, private bathrooms, gourmet dining (2000-5000 MAD/person)

## What to Pack

- Warm layers (desert nights are cold, even in summer)
- Sunscreen and sunglasses
- Scarf for sand protection
- Camera with extra battery
- Headlamp or flashlight
- Comfortable walking shoes

## Best Time for Sahara

October-April. Avoid June-August (temperatures exceed 45°C).
