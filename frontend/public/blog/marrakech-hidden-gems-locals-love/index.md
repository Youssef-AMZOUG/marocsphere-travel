## What Are the Hidden Gems in Marrakech Locals Love?

Every tourist visits Jemaa el-Fnaa and Majorelle Gardens. But Marrakech has layers of hidden beauty that only locals know. Here are the spots we love.

## Secret Gardens & Quiet Corners

**Le Jardin Secret:** Two stunning gardens in the heart of the medina, far quieter than Majorelle. 70 MAD entry.

**Anima Garden:** Andre Heller's artistic paradise 30 minutes outside the city. A surreal experience with sculptures and exotic plants.

**Koutoubia Gardens at dawn:** Come at 6 AM for the most peaceful moment in Marrakech. Only locals and stray cats.

## Local Food Spots (Not on TripAdvisor)

**Chez Bejgueni:** The best tanjia in Marrakech. A tiny shop in the medina where locals queue. 30 MAD for a life-changing meal.

**Cafe des Epices:** Rooftop cafe in the spice souk. Better views and half the price of the famous Cafe de France.

**Souk el Khemis flea market:** Every Thursday. Where Marrakchis shop for antiques, not tourists.

## Artisan Workshops

Visit the **Mouassine fountain area** for contemporary Moroccan design. **Riad Yima** for pop art and vintage tea culture. The **tanneries of Bab Debbagh** (less crowded than Fes tanneries).
