## Is Morocco a Good Destination for Families with Kids?

Absolutely! Morocco is one of the most family-friendly destinations in Africa and the Middle East. Moroccans adore children and welcome families warmly everywhere you go.

## Best Family-Friendly Cities

**Marrakech:** Riads with pools, camel rides at Palmeraie (suitable for kids 6+), Jemaa el-Fnaa performers delight children.

**Essaouira:** Safe beaches, relaxed atmosphere, the medina is small enough for kids to explore.

**Ouarzazate:** Atlas Film Studios — kids love seeing where their favorite movies were made.

## Age-Appropriate Activities

**Under 5:** Pool riads, beach play in Essaouira, gentle donkey rides, Marrakech gardens.

**Ages 6-12:** Camel rides, cooking classes, desert camping (glamping options), pottery workshops.

**Teens:** Surfing in Taghazout, ATVs in the desert, hiking Atlas Mountains, souk bargaining (they love it!).

## Safety Tips for Families

- Avoid the Fes medina with very young children (steep stairs, narrow alleys, motorcycles)
- Bring child-safe sunscreen (Morocco is very sunny)
- Bottled water only
- Most riads can arrange babysitters
