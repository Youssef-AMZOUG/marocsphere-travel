## The Perfect 3-Day Marrakech Itinerary

The perfect 3-day Marrakech itinerary covering Jemaa el-Fnaa, Bahia Palace, Majorelle Garden, souks, and hidden gems. Day-by-day guide with tips and prices.

## Day 1: The Medina & Jemaa el-Fnaa

**Morning:** Start at Jemaa el-Fnaa square. Explore the northern souks — spices, leather, ceramics, and textiles. Bargain starting at 30% of asking price.

**Afternoon:** Bahia Palace (70 MAD) and Saadian Tombs (70 MAD). Walk through the Mellah (Jewish Quarter).

**Evening:** Return to Jemaa el-Fnaa at sunset. Food stalls open at dusk — try snail soup, grilled meats, and fresh orange juice (4 MAD/glass).

## Day 2: Gardens & Culture

**Morning:** Jardin Majorelle (150 MAD). Arrive at 8 AM opening to avoid crowds. The Yves Saint Laurent Museum is next door (30 MAD).

**Afternoon:** Cooking class at a local riad (350-500 MAD). Learn to make tagine, couscous, and Moroccan salads.

**Evening:** Rooftop dinner overlooking the medina. Try Cafe Nomad or Le Jardin.

## Day 3: Hidden Marrakech

**Morning:** Le Jardin Secret (70 MAD). Quieter than Majorelle, equally beautiful.

**Afternoon:** Explore Mouassine quarter for contemporary art and design. Visit Dar Si Said Museum (20 MAD).

**Evening:** Traditional hammam experience (400-600 MAD). The perfect way to end your Marrakech adventure.
