## When Is the Best Time to Visit Morocco?

Discover the perfect time to visit Morocco based on weather, festivals, crowds, and prices. From Marrakech summers to Sahara winters, plan your ideal trip.

## Month-by-Month Guide

**January-February:** Cool and pleasant in cities (15-20°C). Best for Sahara Desert (avoid summer heat). Low season = fewer crowds and better prices.

**March-April:** Spring brings wildflowers to the Atlas Mountains. Ideal temperatures (20-25°C). Ramadan dates vary — check calendar.

**May-June:** Warm but comfortable (25-30°C). Great beach weather in Essaouira and Taghazout. Shoulder season pricing.

**July-August:** Hot in Marrakech and Fes (35-45°C). Best for mountain trekking and coastal towns. Peak European tourism.

**September-October:** Ideal temperatures return (22-28°C). Harvest season — dates, olives, and argan. Best overall travel window.

**November-December:** Mild in cities (15-22°C). Excellent for Sahara trips. Low season pricing. Holiday atmosphere.

## Our Recommendation

**Best overall:** September-November. Perfect weather, fewer crowds, lower prices.
**Best for Sahara:** October-March. Comfortable desert temperatures.
**Best for surfing:** December-March. Atlantic swells peak.
**Best for budget:** January-February, November. Lowest prices everywhere.
