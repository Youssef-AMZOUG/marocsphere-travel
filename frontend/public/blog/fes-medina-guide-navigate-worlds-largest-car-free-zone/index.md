## Fes Medina: Don't Get Lost

Don't get lost in Fes! Expert tips for exploring the medieval medina, tanneries, Al-Qarawiyyin University, and finding the best riads.

## About Fes el-Bali

Fes el-Bali is the world's largest car-free urban area, with over 9,400 alleys and 11,000+ buildings. Founded in the 9th century, it's a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the most intact medieval city in the world.

## Must-See Sights

1. **Al-Qarawiyyin University** — World's oldest operating university (founded 859 AD)
2. **Chouara Tannery** — Iconic leather dyeing pits. Accept the mint sprig!
3. **Bou Inania Madrasa** — Stunning 14th-century Islamic school
4. **Mellah (Jewish Quarter)** — Historic neighborhood with artisan jewelry
5. **Bab Boujloud** — The famous blue gate entrance to the medina

## Navigation Tips

- Hire an official guide for your first day (250-400 MAD/half day)
- Download MarocSphere's offline map
- Remember: downhill leads to the center, uphill leads out
- Major landmarks are signposted with brown tourist signs
- When truly lost, ask a shopkeeper — they're usually helpful

## Best Riads in Fes

Budget: Dar Seffarine (300-500 MAD). Mid-range: Riad Laaroussa (600-1200 MAD). Luxury: Riad Fes (3000+ MAD).

## Fes Specialties

Try **pastilla** (Fes is the original home), **pottery from the Fes kilns**, and **blue ceramics** from the artisan cooperative.
