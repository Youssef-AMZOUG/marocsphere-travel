## Your Morocco Itineraries

Sign in to view your saved AI-generated itineraries. Each itinerary includes:

- Day-by-day activity plans
- Recommended restaurants and riads
- Budget estimates in MAD
- Transportation logistics
- Safety tips per location
- Offline access (Voyager plan)

## Create a New Itinerary

Use the [AI Concierge](/concierge) to generate a new personalized itinerary. Tell the AI your:

1. Destination(s) in Morocco
2. Travel dates and duration
3. Budget level
4. Interests and travel style
5. Group type (solo, couple, family, group)
