# Partner Dashboard - MarocSphere

> Manage your MarocSphere partner account. View bookings, analytics, and reviews from your business dashboard.

**Website:** [https://marocsphere.com/partner/dashboard](https://marocsphere.com/partner/dashboard)

---

## Partner Dashboard

Access your partner dashboard to manage bookings, view analytics, and respond to customer reviews.

### Dashboard Tabs

- **Overview** — Key stats: total bookings, revenue, average rating, views
- **Bookings** — Manage incoming booking requests (accept/decline)
- **Analytics** — Performance metrics, monthly trends, booking sources
- **Reviews** — Read and respond to customer reviews

---

## Features

- Real-time booking notifications
- Revenue tracking in MAD
- Customer review management
- Performance analytics
- Profile management
