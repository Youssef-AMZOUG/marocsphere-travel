## Become a MarocSphere Partner

Join Morocco's largest verified tourism partner network. Register your business to appear in MarocSphere's AI recommendations, interactive map, and travel itineraries.

---

## Partner Types

- **Licensed Guide** — Certified tourist guide
- **Riad / Hotel** — Accommodation provider
- **Restaurant** — Food & dining establishment
- **Activity Provider** — Tours & experiences
- **Transport** — Transfers & car rentals
- **Artisan** — Crafts & workshops

---

## Cities Available

Marrakech, Fes, Chefchaouen, Essaouira, Casablanca, Rabat, Tangier, Agadir, Ouarzazate, Merzouga

---

## Partner Benefits

- Appear in AI-generated itineraries
- Featured on the interactive map
- Direct booking management
- Analytics dashboard
- Verified badge increases trust
- Access to 50,000+ travelers

## Partner Plans

- **Free Listing** — Basic business listing (0 MAD/month)
- **Partner** — Enhanced listing + booking management (490 MAD/month)
- **Partner Pro** — Priority placement + advanced analytics (1490 MAD/month)

---

## Registration Steps

1. Create your account (name, email, password)
2. Enter business details (type, city, description)
3. Provide additional info (languages, experience, website)
4. Submit for verification
5. Get approved by MarocSphere team
