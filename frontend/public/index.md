## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does the AI itinerary planner work?**
Our AI analyzes your travel preferences, budget, interests, and travel dates to create a personalized day-by-day itinerary. It considers local events, weather, and opening hours to optimize your experience.

**Is MarocSphere free to use?**
Yes! Basic itinerary planning and safety features are completely free. Premium features like detailed offline maps and priority support are available with our Pro plan.

**How accurate is the safety information?**
Our safety data is updated in real-time from multiple sources including local authorities, traveler reports, and verified news sources. We refresh safety scores every 15 minutes.

**Can I customize my generated itinerary?**
Absolutely! After the AI generates your initial plan, you can easily swap activities, adjust timing, add or remove destinations, and save multiple versions.

**Does the app work offline?**
Yes, you can download your itinerary and maps for offline access. Safety alerts require an internet connection for real-time updates.
