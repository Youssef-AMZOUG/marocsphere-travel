## Top Destinations in Morocco

### Marrakech
The Red City. Morocco's most vibrant destination with Jemaa el-Fnaa, Bahia Palace, Majorelle Garden, and the famous souks. Safety: Safe. Rating: 4.8/5.
- **Best Time:** October-April
- **Highlights:** Medina, souks, palaces, riads, rooftop restaurants
- [Plan a Marrakech Trip](/concierge?destination=Marrakech) | [View on Map](/map?lat=31.63&lng=-8.0)

### Fes
The Spiritual Capital. Home to the world's oldest university (Al-Qarawiyyin, 859 AD), legendary tanneries, and the most labyrinthine medina in the world. Safety: Caution. Rating: 4.7/5.
- **Best Time:** March-May, September-November
- **Highlights:** Medina, tanneries, Al-Qarawiyyin, ceramics
- [Plan a Fes Trip](/concierge?destination=Fes) | [View on Map](/map?lat=34.03&lng=-5.0)

### Chefchaouen
The Blue Pearl. A stunning blue-washed mountain town in the Rif Mountains. One of the most photographed cities in Africa. Safety: Safe. Rating: 4.9/5.
- **Best Time:** April-June, September-October
- **Highlights:** Blue streets, Kasbah Museum, Spanish Mosque hike
- [Plan a Chefchaouen Trip](/concierge?destination=Chefchaouen) | [View on Map](/map?lat=35.17&lng=-5.27)

### Essaouira
The Windy City. A relaxed coastal town with Portuguese fortifications, art galleries, and world-class seafood. Perfect for families. Safety: Safe. Rating: 4.7/5.
- **Best Time:** June-September
- **Highlights:** Ramparts, beach, port, Gnaoua music, argan oil
- [Plan an Essaouira Trip](/concierge?destination=Essaouira) | [View on Map](/map?lat=31.51&lng=-9.77)

### Merzouga
Gateway to the Sahara. Where Morocco meets the desert. Camel treks, luxury desert camps, and unforgettable sunrises over Erg Chebbi dunes. Safety: Safe. Rating: 4.8/5.
- **Best Time:** October-April
- **Highlights:** Camel treks, desert camps, sandboarding, stargazing
- [Plan a Merzouga Trip](/concierge?destination=Merzouga) | [View on Map](/map?lat=31.1&lng=-4.01)

### Rabat
The Capital. Morocco's administrative capital with a mix of modern and historic attractions. Often overlooked by tourists but rich in culture. Safety: Safe. Rating: 4.5/5.
- **Best Time:** Year-round
- **Highlights:** Hassan Tower, Kasbah of the Udayas, Chellah ruins
- [Plan a Rabat Trip](/concierge?destination=Rabat) | [View on Map](/map?lat=34.02&lng=-6.83)
